Nomad-Ready? Check

Check if you meet the basic requirements for Japan's Digital Nomad Visa with these simple "Yes/No" questions.

1. Is your nationality eligible?

Citizens of countries with visa exemption agreements and tax treaties with Japan are eligible.

2. Is your annual income at least JPY 10 million?

Your individual annual income must meet the threshold when converted to JPY.

3. Are you a remote worker?

You must be employed by a company outside Japan or work for clients abroad.

4. Do you have private health insurance?

Insurance must cover medical expenses during your stay for at least JPY 10 million.

The result will be displayed once you answer "Yes" to all questions.
